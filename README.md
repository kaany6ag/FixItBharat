## Problem Statement
Community Hero — Hyperlocal Problem Solver
Vibe2Ship Hackathon 2026

## Solution Overview
FixItBharat is an AI-powered civic issue reporting 
platform where citizens upload a photo of any civic 
problem and Gemini AI instantly analyzes it, 
categorizes it, scores severity, and files a formal 
complaint automatically. No forms. No offices. 
60 seconds. Fully autonomous.

## Key Features
- Gemini Vision photo analysis + severity scoring
- Repair History Intelligence + Recurrence Risk Score
- Smart Escalation Engine Day 7 to 15 to 30
- RTI Auto-Draft under RTI Act 2005
- Monsoon Vulnerability Prediction Map
- Department + Officer Performance Scorecards
- Shame Board for public accountability
- Hindi Voice Complaints via Web Speech API
- Sachcha Nagrik Reward System + Honesty Score
- FixBot AI Chatbot Assistant
- Nearby Issues Feed 2km radius
- Success Wall before and after gallery
- SOS Emergency Alert severity 9 and 10
- Weekly Ward Newsletter by Gemini
- 50+ Indian cities supported
- Bilingual English and Hindi

## Agentic Loop
Photo uploaded
→ Gemini Vision analyzes automatically
→ Auto categorizes and routes to department
→ Community verifies
→ Day 7 auto escalates to Senior Officer
→ Day 15 escalates to District Collector
→ Day 30 RTI drafts itself automatically
→ Shame Board publishes automatically
Zero human moderation. Fully autonomous.
